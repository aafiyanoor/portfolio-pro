Digital Marketing Overview

Facebook Ads (Meta Ads)

Facebook Ads, now under Meta Ads, allows businesses to reach users on Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, and Audience Network through highly targeted campaigns.

Key Features:

  • Audience Targeting: Location, age, gender, interests, behaviors
  • Ad Formats: Image, video, carousel, collection, stories, reels
  • Campaign Objectives: Awareness, traffic, engagement, leads, sales
  • Budgeting: Daily or lifetime budget, manual/automatic bidding

Advantages:

  • Huge user base
  • Granular targeting
  • Strong visual formats
  • Instagram integration

Google Ads (GAM - Google Ads Manager)

Google Ads lets you run ads across Search, YouTube, Display Network, Gmail, and Shopping.

Campaign Types:

  • Search Ads
  • Display Ads
  • Shopping Ads
  • Video Ads
  • Performance Max

Key Features:

  • Keyword targeting
  • Demographic/location targeting
  • Ad extensions
  • Smart bidding strategies

Benefits:

  • High-intent search traffic
  • Large audience reach
  • Real-time tracking

Reporting & Analytics

Reporting is crucial to track digital marketing performance and optimize campaigns.

Common Metrics:

  • Impressions, Clicks, CTR
  • Conversions, Conversion Rate
  • Cost per Click (CPC)
  • Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)
  • Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
  • Engagement metrics

Tools Used:

  • Google Analytics / GA4
  • Meta Ads Manager
  • Google Ads Dashboard
  • Google Looker Studio
  • UTM Parameters

Tips:

  • Use visual dashboards
  • Track KPIs by campaign goals
  • Analyze trends over time
