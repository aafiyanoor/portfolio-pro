1.BrandBoost Social Media Campaign

1. Description:

Spearheaded the full-cycle strategy and execution of a high-impact multi-platform social media campaign for a leading fashion brand. The project targeted Gen Z and Millennial audiences, resulting in a 35% increase in engagement and a 22% rise in sales inquiries over a 3-month period.

2. Objectives:

  • Boost brand awareness across Instagram, Facebook, and Pinterest.
  • Drive traffic to the new seasonal product landing page.
  • Grow authentic audience interaction and customer loyalty.

3. Skills Used:

Social Media Strategy, Content Creation, Facebook Ads, Instagram Growth, Paid Media Management, Audience Research, Analytics & Reporting.

4. Highlights:

  • 📅 Content Planning: Designed detailed monthly content calendars tailored for each platform, ensuring consistency with brand voice and aesthetics.
  • 🎯 Targeted Advertising: Managed a Facebook and Instagram ads budget of $5,000, optimizing ad sets based on A/B testing and real-time data to achieve a 3.8x return on ad spend (ROAS).
  • 📊 Performance Analysis: Monitored KPIs like reach, impressions, CTR, and conversions weekly, preparing strategic reports for stakeholders.
  • 🧠 Creative Campaigns: Launched a user-generated content (UGC) contest which resulted in 500+ organic shares and 100+ customer-created posts.
  • 🛠️ Tools Used: Facebook Business Manager, Instagram Insights, Canva for creatives, Later.com for scheduling, Google Analytics.

5. Outcome:

  • 35% increase in social media engagement.
  • 22% increase in website inquiries.
  • 18% growth in Instagram followers over 3 months.
  • Successfully aligned the campaign with the brand's seasonal collection launch.

2.SEO Optimization for TechNova

1. Description:

Led a full-scale SEO optimization project for TechNova, a fast-growing technology solutions company. The goal was to increase organic visibility, improve keyword rankings, and drive high-quality traffic to the website, resulting in a 45% increase in organic traffic and a 30% rise in qualified leads within 6 months.

2. Objectives:

  • Improve keyword rankings for highly competitive industry terms.
  • Enhance on-page SEO and overall website structure.
  • Boost organic traffic and lead generation through strategic content marketing.

3. Skills Used:

SEO Strategy, Keyword Research, On-Page SEO, Content Optimization, Technical SEO, Link Building, Analytics & Reporting, Google Search Console, SEMrush.

4. Highlights:

  • 📄 Website Audit: Conducted an in-depth SEO audit identifying critical issues affecting ranking and visibility.
  • 🔑 Keyword Strategy: Built a robust keyword map targeting both short-tail and long-tail keywords relevant to TechNova’s services.
  • 🛠️ Technical SEO Fixes: Optimized website loading speed, mobile responsiveness, and resolved indexing issues.
  • ✍️ Content Creation: Developed optimized blog posts and updated service pages to match search intent and improve engagement.
  • 🔗 Backlink Campaign: Secured high-authority backlinks through guest posting, partnerships, and PR outreach.
  • 📈 Analytics Monitoring: Tracked and reported monthly performance metrics (traffic, rankings, CTR) and made data-driven improvements.

5. Outcome:

  • 45% increase in organic website traffic.
  • 30% growth in qualified inbound leads.
  • 20+ high-priority keywords ranked on Google's first page.
  • Enhanced domain authority and brand trust in the technology solutions sector.
